## ✈️ 240‑Hour Visa‑Free Transit by Region (10 Days)
<div className="info-card">
  <h3>🚀 Upgraded Transit Policy</h3>
  <p>
    China has upgraded its <a href="https://en.nia.gov.cn/n147413/c183100/content.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">transit without visa (TWOV) policy</a> to allow 
    <strong>240-hour (10 days)</strong> visa-free stays for passport holders from 55 countries, 
    when transiting to a third country via 60 designated ports in 24 provinces.
  </p>
</div>

### ✅ Key Requirements
<div className="info-grid">
  <div className="visa-type-card">
    #### 🔁 Transit Requirement
    <p>
      You must be <strong>in transit to a third country</strong> (your arrival and departure countries must be different).
    </p>
    <p>
      ✅ <strong>Example:</strong> Japan → Shanghai → South Korea<br />
      ❌ <strong>Not allowed:</strong> Japan → Shanghai → Japan
    </p>
  </div>
  <div className="visa-type-card">
    <h4>🎟️ Documentation</h4>
    <p>You must have:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Valid passport (minimum 3 months validity)</li>
      <li>Confirmed <strong>onward ticket</strong> to a third country within 240 hours</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div className="visa-type-card">
    <h4>🛃 Entry Process</h4>
    <p>
      Complete a temporary arrival card and <strong>undergo border inspection</strong> at entry.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div className="visa-type-card">
    <h4>🚆 Travel Freedom</h4>
    <p>
      You can travel <strong>between provinces</strong> if they are included in the designated 24‑region scope.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

#### 🕒 How Is Time Calculated?
<div className="time-calculation"> <p><strong>The 240 hours start at midnight following your entry</strong></p> <p>🗓 <strong>For example:</strong> You arrive on <strong>Sep 5 at 17:00</strong><br/> → Clock starts at <strong>00:00 on Sep 6</strong><br/> → Must leave by <strong>00:00 on Sep 16</strong></p> </div>

## 🛂 30‑Day Visa-Free Entry (Unilateral Policy)

<div className="info-card">
  <h3>🎉 Great News for Travelers!</h3>
  <p>China currently grants <strong>30-day visa-free stays</strong> for citizens of 30+ countries. This applies to tourism, business, and visiting friends.</p>
</div>

#### ✅ Key Features

<div className="info-grid">
  <div className="visa-type-card">
    <h4>🗺️ Travel Freedom</h4>
    <p>You may travel freely across Mainland China</p>
  </div>
  <div className="visa-type-card">
    <h4>🛂 No Transit Requirement</h4>
    <p>No transit or third-country requirement</p>
  </div>
  <div className="visa-type-card">
    <h4>📅 Duration</h4>
    <p>Maximum stay: <strong>30 natural days</strong></p>
  </div>
</div>

#### 📝 Entry Requirements:

- A valid passport (suggested minimum 6 months validity)
- No visa required upon arrival
- Carry backup documents (recommended):
  - Flight ticket
  - Hotel reservation
  - Invitation letter (optional)

#### 🕒 How Is Time Counted?

<div className="time-calculation">
  <p><strong>Starts from 00:00 the day after entry</strong></p>
  <p>🗓 <strong>Example:</strong> Arrive on Sep 1 → stay valid until Oct 2 at 00:00</p>
</div>
