## â“ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Can I get a visa on arrival in China?

Generally, **no**. China does not offer visas on arrival for most travelers. There are very limited exceptions for emergencies.

### Can I enter with a Hong Kong visa?

**No.** Mainland China, Hong Kong, and Macao have separate immigration systems. You need a separate visa for Mainland China.

### Can I extend my visa while in China?

Yes, visa extensions are possible through the **local Entry-Exit Bureau**, but approval is not guaranteed.

### What happens if I overstay my visa?

Overstaying is a serious offense and may result in:

- Fines (Â¥500/day, up to Â¥10,000)
