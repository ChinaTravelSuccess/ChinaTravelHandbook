<div className="hero-midnight">

    <p className="hero-subtitle">Your passport to China's cashless society</p>
  </div>

<details>
<summary className="custom-summary blue">
<span>🔵 Alipay Setup Guide - International Users</span>
</summary>

### Step-by-Step Alipay Registration

**Step 1: Download & Register**
- Download "Alipay" from official app stores
- Register with your international phone number
- Receive SMS verification code

**Step 2: Critical Passport Verification**
<div className="online-option">
<span className="online-icon">🔑</span>
<strong>This step is MANDATORY for raising transaction limits and accessing full features.</strong>
</div>

1. Tap **<span className="chinese-phrase">Me (我的)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Settings (设置)</span>**
2. Select **<span className="chinese-phrase">Account and Security (账户与安全)</span>**
3. Tap **<span className="chinese-phrase">Identity Information (身份信息)</span>**
4. Upload clear passport photo + facial verification
5. Wait for approval (usually instant)

**Step 3: Link International Card**
- Go to **<span className="chinese-phrase">Me (我的)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Bank Cards (银行卡)</span>**
- Add Visa, Mastercard, JCB, or Discover card
- Complete bank verification (OTP code)

<div className="payment-table-container">
| Card Type | Transaction Limit | Annual Limit | Fees |
|-----------|------------------|--------------|------|
| **Visa/Mastercard** | US$5,000 | US$50,000 | Bank foreign transaction fee |
| **JCB** | US$5,000 | US$50,000 | Bank foreign transaction fee |
| **Discover** | US$5,000 | US$50,000 | Bank foreign transaction fee |
</div>

**Backup Option: Tour Pass**
If direct card linking fails, use Alipay's "Tour Pass" - a prepaid wallet you can top up (may have 5% fee).

</details>

<details>
<summary className="custom-summary lime">
<span>🟢 WeChat Pay Setup Guide - International Users</span>
</summary>

### Step-by-Step WeChat Pay Registration

**Step 1: Download & Register**
- Download "WeChat" from official app stores
- Register with international phone number

**Step 2: Activate Wallet Feature**
The payment feature may be hidden by default on international accounts:

1. Go to **<span className="chinese-phrase">Me (我的)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Services (服务)</span>**
2. Look for **<span className="chinese-phrase">Wallet (钱包)</span>**
3. If not visible: **<span className="chinese-phrase">Me (我的)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Settings (设置)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">General (通用)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Tools (工具)</span>** → Enable WeChat Pay

**Step 3: Identity & Card Verification**
1. **<span className="chinese-phrase">Me (我的)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Services (服务)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Wallet (钱包)</span>**
2. Tap **<span className="chinese-phrase">Cards (银行卡)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Add Card (添加银行卡)</span>**
3. Upload passport details and add international card
4. Set 6-digit payment password

</summary>

### Understanding Transit QR Codes

**Critical Point:** Transit QR codes are **different** from regular payment QR codes. You must activate a city-specific transit pass in your payment app.

### Alipay Transit Setup

1. Open Alipay homepage
2. Tap **<span className="chinese-phrase">Transport (出行)</span>** icon
3. App auto-detects your city (or manually select)
4. Tap **Activate Transit Card** for that city
5. Agree to terms and authorize automatic payments
6. Your city-specific QR code is now ready

### WeChat Transit Setup

1. **<span className="chinese-phrase">Me (我的)</span>** → **<span className="chinese-phrase">Services (服务)</span>**
2. Find **<span className="chinese-phrase">Public Transport (公共交通)</span>**
3. Launch transit mini-program
4. Select your city and activate
5. Authorize automatic fare deductions

### Using Your Transit Code

**Metro Stations:**
- **Entry:** Scan QR code at turnstile
- **Exit:** Scan again - fare automatically calculated
- **No physical tickets needed**

**Buses:**
- **Board:** Scan QR code at front door reader
- **Exit:** Some cities require exit scan

## 🚄 Step 4: Long-Distance Travel - Trains & Flights

<div className="region-card">

China's high-speed rail network is a marvel of modern engineering. Understanding the booking system and real-name requirements is crucial for seamless travel.

</div>

<details>
<summary className="custom-summary orange">
<span>🚄 High-Speed Rail Booking (12306 System)</span>
</summary>

### The 12306 Challenge & Solutions

**The Reality:** 12306 is the ONLY official platform for train tickets, but passport verification can be problematic.

### Plan A: Official English Method

**Step 1: Download & Register**
1. Download "12306" app from official stores
2. Switch language to English
3. Register with email and passport details

**Step 2: Passport Verification**
1. Upload passport main page photo
2. Take selfie holding passport
3. Wait 3-5 business days for manual review
4. **Problem:** Often fails or takes too long

### Plan B: The "Insider" Workaround

<div className="online-option">
<span className="online-icon">🔧</span>
<strong>User-Reported Success:</strong> The Chinese version often provides instant verification.
</div>

**Process:**
1. Switch 12306 app to Chinese language
2. Use Google Translate camera to navigate
3. Upload same passport photos through Chinese interface
4. **Result:** Many users report instant "Verified" status

### Booking Your Train Ticket

**Once Verified:**
1. Search routes with From/To stations and date
2. Choose train (G = High-speed, D = Fast, C = Intercity)
3. Select seat class and preferences
4. Add verified passengers
5. Pay with international card

**Seat Classes:**
- **Second Class (二等座):** 2+3 seating, most popular
- **First Class (一等座):** 2+2 seating, more space
- **Business Class (商务座):** 1+2 seating, premium service

### Boarding Process

<div className="legal-requirement">
  <div className="requirement-icon">🔑</div>
  <div className="requirement-content">
    <h3>Your Passport IS Your Ticket</h3>
    <p>China uses e-tickets. No paper tickets needed - just your original passport for verification at station gates.</p>
  </div>
</div>

**At the Station:**
1. Arrive 30-60 minutes early
2. Go to ticket verification gates
3. Present passport to staff or automated scanner
4. System confirms your booking and lets you through

</details>

<details>
<summary className="custom-summary blue">
<span>✈️ Domestic Flights - Booking Strategy</span>
</summary>

### When to Fly vs. Train

**Choose Train For:**
- City pairs under 800km
- Routes with direct high-speed rail
- City center to city center travel

**Choose Flight For:**
- Distances over 800km
- No direct rail connections
- Time-sensitive travel

### Flight Booking Strategy

**Recommended Platform: Trip.com**
- Polished English interface
- Accepts international payment
- 24/7 English customer support
- Better than airline websites for foreigners

**Alternative: Airline Direct**
- Air China, China Southern, China Eastern
- Often clunky English interfaces
- Limited customer service in English

### Airport Navigation

**Check-in Requirements:**
- Arrive 2 hours early for domestic flights
- Present same passport used for booking
- Standard security procedures

**Power Bank Rules:**
- Must be in carry-on (not checked luggage)
- Capacity limit: 27,000mAh or 160Wh
- Must have readable capacity labels

## 🆔 Understanding Real-Name Registration

<div className="hero-midnight">
  <div className="hero-content">

    <p className="hero-subtitle">Your master key to China's transportation network</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div className="region-card">

### The "Passport as Master Key" Principle

China's real-name registration system (<span className="chinese-phrase">实名制</span>) links your identity to all transportation activities. Your passport is the **single most important document** for travel within China.

</div>

<details>
<summary className="custom-summary lime">
<span>🔑 Real-Name Requirements by Transport Type</span>
</summary>

<div className="payment-table-container">
| Transport Mode | Booking Online | Booking Counter | Boarding/Entry |
|----------------|----------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Domestic Flight** | Passport Details | Passport | Passport |
| **High-Speed Train** | Passport (after 12306 verification) | Passport | Passport |
| **Long-Distance Bus** | Not available for foreigners | Passport | Passport |
| **Metro/City Bus** | Passport (for initial app verification) | N/A | QR Code |
| **Ride-Hailing** | Passport (for payment app verification) | N/A | Phone verification |
</div>

### Key Points

**For Booking:**
- Name must match passport exactly
- Passport number required
- No variations or nicknames accepted

**For Boarding:**
- Original physical passport required
- Digital copies not accepted
- Check expiry date before travel

**For Apps:**
- Passport used for initial verification
- Enables higher transaction limits
- Required for premium features

</details>

<details>
<summary className="custom-summary rose">
<span>🏨 Accommodation Registration</span>
</summary>

### Legal Requirement

**The Rule:** All foreign visitors must register with local police within 24 hours of arrival in any new city.

**Hotels:** Handle registration automatically at check-in
**Private Accommodation:** You and host must register at local police station in person

**Required Documents:**
- Original passport
- Entry stamp or visa
- Accommodation address
- Host's ID (if staying privately)

<div className="online-option">
<span className="online-icon">⚠️</span>
<strong>Important:</strong> This is a legal requirement, not optional. Hotels make it seamless, but private stays require manual registration.
</div>

</details>
