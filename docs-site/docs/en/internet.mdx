## 🚧 The Great Firewall
China's internet landscape is unique due to the Great Firewall.
<div class="block-section">
  <div class="card warning">
    <h3>❌ Blocked Services</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Facebook, Instagram, Twitter</li>
      <li>Google (Search, Maps, Gmail)</li>
      <li>WhatsApp, Telegram, YouTube</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="card info">
    <h3>✅ Alternatives in China</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Search:</strong> Baidu, Bing (China)</li>
      <li><strong>Maps:</strong> Amap (高德), Baidu Maps</li>
      <li><strong>Messaging:</strong> WeChat, QQ</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

## 📱 Mobile Data & SIM Options

Choose what suits you best:

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="foreign" label="🌍 Foreign SIM + Roaming" default>

<strong>Pros:</strong><br />
✅ Keep your original number<br />
✅ Seamless access to Gmail, Google Maps, WhatsApp<br />
⚠️ Expensive (e.g., $10/day)<br /><br />

<strong>Cons:</strong><br />
❌ May not work for local services (e.g., train bookings)

  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="esim" label="📲 eSIM (International)">

<strong>Pros:</strong><br />
✅ Easy to buy via apps (Airalo, Nomad, Holafly)<br />
✅ No physical SIM needed<br />
✅ Allows VPN to be preconfigured<br /><br />

<strong>How to Get:</strong><br />
🛒 Purchase through platforms like <Badge type="info">Nomad</Badge>, <Badge type="info">airalo</Badge>, or <Badge type="info">SimOptions</Badge><br />
📱 Install the corresponding app and select a "China" or "Asia" package<br />
⚙️ Follow app instructions to install the eSIM profile (recommended before arrival)<br /><br />

<strong>Cons:</strong><br />
❌ No Chinese phone number<br />
❌ Can’t receive local SMS<br />
❌ Slightly slower than physical SIMs

  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="local" label="🇨🇳 Chinese SIM Card">

<strong>Pros:</strong><br />
✅ Fast 4G/5G data, wide coverage<br />
✅ Local number (calls, SMS)<br />
✅ Full compatibility with Chinese apps (Didi, Meituan, Alipay)<br /><br />

<strong>How to Get:</strong><br />
🧳 Bring your <Badge type="info">passport</Badge> as it's required for ID verification<br />
🛬 Get one right after landing at an <Badge type="warning">airport telecom counter</Badge> (more expensive but English-friendly)<br />
🏙️ Or visit an <Badge type="warning">official store in the city</Badge> (often Chinese-only service)<br />
⚠️ Always ask for a <Badge type="danger">new number</Badge> to avoid recycled SIMs that may be linked to previous users’ apps/accounts<br /><br />

## 🛬 Airport Wi-Fi & SIM Access

<div class="tip-box info">
  Most major airports in China offer **free Wi‑Fi**. You may need to:
  - Scan your passport
  - Receive a temporary code via SMS or kiosk
</div>

## 🧠 Pro Tips

- 📲 Install apps like WeChat, Alipay, Didi, and Baidu Maps *before* arrival  
- 🔒 Save VPN setup files offline  
- 🔤 Download translation apps or phrasebooks (Google Translate, Pleco)  
- 💡 Combine a Chinese SIM + VPN for optimal performance
