## 🛂 Foreigner Stay Registration
<div class="registration-section">
  <div class="legal-requirement">
    <div class="requirement-icon">⚖️</div>
    <div class="requirement-content">
      <h3>Legal Requirement</h3>
      <p><strong>All foreigners must register their accommodation within 24 hours of arrival</strong> — whether hotel, rental, or a friend's home. This is mandatory in China.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="registration-options">
    <div class="option-card automatic">
      <div class="option-header">
        <span class="option-icon">🏨</span>
        <h4>Hotels or Licensed Rentals</h4>
        <span class="difficulty-badge easy">Easy</span>
      </div>
      <p>Check-in staff will register you automatically using your passport — no extra steps required.</p>
    </div>
<div class="option-card manual">
  <div class="option-header">
    <span class="option-icon">🏠</span>
    <h4>Private Home Stays</h4>
    <span class="difficulty-badge moderate">Manual</span>
  </div>
  <p>You must register manually at the local police station or immigration office.</p>
  <div class="required-docs">
    <h5>Required Documents:</h5>
    <ul>
      <li>📖 Your passport</li>
      <li>🆔 Host's Chinese ID</li>
      <li>📋 Property ownership certificate (房产证)</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="online-option">
    <span class="online-icon">📱</span>
    <strong>Online Option:</strong> Some cities allow registration via "National Immigration" WeChat mini-program
  </div>
</div>
  </div>
</div>

## 📋 Hotel Guest Data Reporting
<div class="info-section">
  <div class="info-grid">
    <div class="info-item">
      <span class="info-icon">🏢</span>
      <h4>Hotel Responsibility</h4>
      <p>Hotels are legally required to report guest information to the Public Security Bureau (PSB)</p>
    </div>
    <div class="info-item">
      <span class="info-icon">🌐</span>
      <h4>International Chains</h4>
      <p>Most international hotel chains handle this process automatically</p>
    </div>
    <div class="info-item">
      <span class="info-icon">⚠️</span>
      <h4>Local Properties</h4>
      <p>Smaller properties may be unfamiliar with foreign guest procedures</p>
    </div>
    <div class="info-item">
      <span class="info-icon">⏰</span>
      <h4>Time Limit</h4>
      <p>Always ensure registration is completed within 24 hours to avoid fines</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

## 💡 Essential Pro Tips
<div class="tips-container">
  <div class="tip-category">
    <h4>🚀 Before You Go</h4>
    <ul class="tips-list">
      <li>Download booking apps before arriving in China</li>
      <li>Take a photo of your passport and visa</li>
      <li>Set up a VPN for international booking apps</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="tip-category">
    <h4>🗣️ Communication</h4>
    <ul class="tips-list">
      <li>Learn this phrase: <span class="chinese-phrase">"你们可以接待外宾吗？"</span><br/>
          <span class="pinyin">(Nǐmen kěyǐ jiēdài wàibīn ma?)</span><br/>
          <span class="translation">"Can you accommodate foreign guests?"</span></li>
      <li>Look for filters like "foreigners welcome" or "international guests"</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="tip-category">
    <h4>📋 Documentation</h4>
    <ul class="tips-list">
      <li>Keep digital and printed copies of your booking</li>
      <li>If staying privately, register with police ASAP</li>
      <li>Save important documents in cloud storage</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
